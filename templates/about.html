Mission statement:

We believe safer roads start with better understanding. Every statistic represents a real event on our roads, which is why we make crash data accessible, clear, and meaningful. By helping Victorians understand trends, identify risks, and explore the factors behind road incidents, we empower informed decisions that contribute to safer communities and roads across Victoria.


How to use this website:

Accident Condition

Explore how weather, lighting, road surface, and other environmental factors relate to road accidents. Use the available filters to compare different conditions and discover patterns across incidents.

People and Injury

Investigate injury outcomes and the people involved in road accidents using interactive filters and selection options. Refine the data to focus on specific injury types, severity levels, or outcomes.

Accident Analysis

Search and analyse individual accident records using postcode filters, detailed datasets, and interactive visualisations. This page is ideal for exploring accident trends within specific locations.

People Analysis

Examine demographic information about people involved in road incidents. Use postcode and data filters to explore trends and gain deeper insights into different groups affected by road accidents.
